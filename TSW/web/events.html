The Journey So Far

What began as just an idea has till date been an enticing journey with unforgettable memories collected and lessons learned along the way. A vivid display of the happenings, conducted under the banner of The Soul Within goes as-

Open mic 1.0

3rd February was that auspicious day when The SoulWithin conducted it's first ever open mic session, which was attended by more than 40 performers of different genres to showcase their brilliance and enthrall the audience. This event witnessed the presence of RJ Ishank and Hindi poet Ramakant Singh "Neelkanth" as it's guests. To whom we expressed our deepest gratitude. The team also is beholden to the accomplished performers who turned that evening into mesmerizing one. And we thus took the very first step of a very long journey to come.

Open mic 2.0

The slow steps turned into sharp leap with the conduct of the second OpenMic Session administered by The Soulwithin at Cafe Unplugged. The event was once again graced with the presence of eminent poets and storytellers from all across the town and nearby. Also, Mr. Monis Ahmed Khan, ab established poet, story-teller and YouTuber embellished the event with his presence as the Chief Guest and his words of astuteness surely stole the show. It being the International Women's Day, the team gifted all the wome present in the event with tokens of love and respect.

Nazm

After a series of successful offline events, next in the que came the first online event by The Soulwithin ,the quote writing competition titled "Nazm". This event garnered lots of attention and has been in talks for quite sometime. Those who really stirred the soul among the lot were shortlisted for a community, to be an integral part of The Soulwithin and be among the best of thinkers. Along with this the team provided other benefits to the deserving winners of the competition. And so the first online event reached out to a bigger crowd and won many hearts

Kavyotsav

After two successful Open Mic Sessions came the much anticipated event "Kavyotsav",the poetical hangout organized by TheSoulWithin under the aegis of "Pravah: AKTU's Art And Cultural Fest" which took stage with great pomp and show. Member of the Writer's Hub Kenashree, multitalented YouTuber Mr. Monis Ahmed Khan and the breathtaking performance of Aayush Sehta and band took the stage to another level.

Pratibimb

In the row of successful events organized by The Soul Within next came, "Pratibimb" conducted on 10th of April in the auditorium of IET, Lucknow. Yet again, the charmers, Ayush Sehta, Violinist, Gagan Walia, Guitarist and Beatboxer Siddhartha performed their catchy tunes to set the stage on fire. Also, time now was to go a little out of the box, as we came up with two successful Entrepreneurs Stuti and Arpita to talk on running an art based hangout location "Cafe Asante" in Lucknow. And the very popular RJ Prateek from BBD FM 90.8, entertained the audience with his wits. In all, Pratibimb went as a huge success and is remembered as a memorable experience. And thus, TheSoulWithin kept on to scale newer heights

कविता संवाद: हिंदी साहित्यिक कार्यशाला

Completing yet another accolade to the poetic journey ,The Soulwithin organized it's first workshop on 9th June at Jail Cave Cafe, Lucknow with great composites. This exhilarating workshop made it's audience learn about Hindi literature and content writing skills by renowned and well established personality Dr.Dheeraj Mishra, a celebrated writer and poet honored by distinguished stages of poetry and prose. The event threw light on major insights of poetry writing and comprehension.This successful workshop was attended by an interactive bunch of listeners who dream to make it big in art and literature.Thus adding another feather to the cap of TheSoulWithin
